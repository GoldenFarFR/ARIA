## Question
What is the ARIA capability index (QI)?

## Answer
The ARIA Index measures capability across 6 axes (coding, social, intelligence, reliability, autonomy, business), each from level 0 to 1000. Level 1 objectives can be completed in about one day; level 1000 takes years. Progress is evidence-based — operator validates via /level up on Telegram or POST /api/aria/capability/level-up. Business levels auto-advance when real revenue in revenue_ledger meets targets. SSOT rubric capability_rubric.yaml.
