## Question
What is Aria Vanguard ZHC?

## Answer
Aria Vanguard ZHC is the ZHC parent holding. Every current and future venture registers as a subsidiary under the holding — never the holding itself. No subsidiary is currently live: ARIA operates the holding directly at https://ariavanguardzhc.com. Aria Market and DEXPulse are retired codenames, not live products — never present either as a current subsidiary.
